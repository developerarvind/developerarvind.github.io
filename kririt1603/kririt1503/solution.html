DigiMarking

Marking paper and entering results on excel spreadsheet is a time-consuming process. Also circulating paper to multiple markers is complex and inefficient, as well as risky from a security point-of-view. It’s time to change the manual exams marking system to the Digital Marking solution that can streamline your exam administration.
Examining bodies needs to maintain a consistent and high-quality marking solution which is beneficial to reduce the number of scripts being sent back for review. DigiMarking Solution will always enable examining bodies to monitor and keep track of markers throughout the marking process and control at all level..

Features and Functionality

  • Digitalization of answer sheets – Bar-coding and Scanning
  • Live Marking
