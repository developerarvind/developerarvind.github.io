Course Info

Web design encompasses many different skills and disciplines in the production and maintenance of websites. Web Designing course is designed to give the understanding of technology and platform used to create modern and responsive website.

In a nutshell Web designing is an art and science of building the look and feel as well as how website will function. Designing a website which should be clear, concise, and easy to use will ultimately lead to a better user experience for your target audience.

The course provides a comprehensive view of web design lay out, styling, debugging and manipulating websites with a focus on design.
